Widows Programme

Help The Widows Ministry

Our Widows MInistry is dedicated to embodying the essence of James 1:27 by actively supporting widows within church communities, tending to their immediate physical requirements, and guiding them towards a more profound and personal relationship with Jesus Christ. This program no only addresses the pressing and current needs of these widows but also places a strong emphasis on establishing sustainable income opportunities to secure their future financial stability. Through the collaborative efforts with our great communities, local churches, and pastors, the ministry not only empowers widows to become self-sufficient but also fosters a sense of community and mutual support amont the members involved in the program.
